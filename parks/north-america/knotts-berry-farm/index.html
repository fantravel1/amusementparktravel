Knott's Berry Farm: The Complete 2025 Guide

Knott's Berry Farm is America's first theme park, predating Disneyland by 15 years. What started as a roadside berry stand grew into a Western-themed attraction that pioneered the theme park concept. Today it delivers excellent coasters, genuine history, and America's best Halloween event.

The Heritage

The Knott family began selling berries and chicken dinners during the Great Depression. The themed Ghost Town grew to entertain waiting diners. By the time Disneyland opened in 1955, Knott's had been running themed entertainment for over a decade.

Key Stats:

  • Since 1940 — America's first theme park
  • 10 roller coasters including GhostRider
  • Ghost Town — Original themed area
  • Scary Farm — #1 Halloween event in America

Signature Coasters

GhostRider — Wooden Legend

One of the world's best wooden coasters:

  • Type: CCI wooden (GCI retracked)
  • Length: 4,533 feet
  • Speed: 56 mph
  • Height Requirement: 48"
  • Experience: Relentless airtime after 2016 retrack

HangTime — Dive Into the Night

California's first dive coaster:

  • Type: Gerstlauer Infinity (dive)
  • Drop: 150 feet at 96 degrees (beyond vertical)
  • Hold: Suspense before the drop
  • Height Requirement: 48"
  • Experience: Unique, intense

Xcelerator — Launch Legend

Intamin accelerator:

  • Launch: 0-82 mph in 2.3 seconds
  • Height: 205 feet
  • Type: Hydraulic launch
  • Height Requirement: 52"
  • Experience: Raw speed and power

Frequently Asked Questions

How does it compare to Disneyland?

Completely different. Knott's is a traditional amusement park with better coasters and genuine history. Disneyland is immersive fantasy. Both worth visiting — only 10 minutes apart.

Is GhostRider really that good?

Yes. After the 2016 GCI retrack, it became one of the world's best wooden coasters.

One day enough?

Yes, easily. Less crowded than Six Flags Magic Mountain, more manageable.

Is Scary Farm actually scary?

Yes. Professional actors, elaborate mazes, genuine intensity. Not for young children or the easily frightened.

Knott's Berry Farm occupies a unique place in theme park history — the genuine original that keeps evolving. The combination of authentic heritage, excellent coasters, and America's best Halloween event makes it essential California entertainment.
