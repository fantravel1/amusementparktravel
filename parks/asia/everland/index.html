Everland: The Complete 2025 Guide

Everland is South Korea's largest and most visited theme park, attracting over 6 million visitors annually with its exceptional coasters, beautiful seasonal gardens, and genuine safari experience. Owned by Samsung, it delivers Korean efficiency with world-class attractions.

Why Everland Stands Out

As Korea's flagship theme park, Everland combines a diverse attraction mix with stunning seasonal landscaping and K-pop cultural events. T Express, the wooden coaster, ranks among Asia's best.

Key Stats:

  • 370 acres of themed zones
  • 40+ attractions including world-class coasters
  • Lost Valley — Real safari experience
  • Seasonal gardens — Tulips, roses, and more

Signature Attractions

T Express — Asia's Best Wooden Coaster

World-class wooden coaster:

  • Type: Intamin wooden coaster
  • Height: 56 meters (184 feet)
  • Speed: 104 km/h (65 mph)
  • Drop: 77 degrees — Steepest wooden when built
  • Height Requirement: 130cm
  • Experience: Intense, fast, exceptional airtime

Thunder Falls — World's Steepest Water Ride

Record-breaking plunge:

  • Drop Angle: 70 degrees
  • Height: 20 meters
  • Splash: Massive (poncho essential)
  • Experience: Quick but spectacular

Lost Valley — Live Safari

Real African animals:

  • Experience: Vehicle safari through habitats
  • Animals: Lions, elephants, giraffes, and more
  • Feeding: Opportunities to feed giraffes
  • Included: With park admission

Best Time to Visit

Crowd Patterns

  • Lowest: Weekdays in March, November
  • Moderate: Spring weekdays, fall weekdays
  • Busiest: Korean holidays, weekends, summer

Korean Holidays (Very Busy)

  • Seollal (Lunar New Year)
  • Chuseok (Korean Thanksgiving)
  • Children's Day (May 5)

Weather

  • Spring (April-May): Perfect — tulip festival
  • Summer: Hot and humid
  • Fall (Sept-Oct): Ideal temperatures
  • Winter: Cold but beautiful illuminations

Frequently Asked Questions

Everland vs Lotte World?

Everland is larger, outdoor, better coasters, real safari. Lotte World is indoor, convenient (Seoul metro), smaller. Enthusiasts prefer Everland; Seoul tourists often do Lotte for convenience.

Is T Express world-class?

Absolutely. It ranks among the world's best wooden coasters and is easily Asia's finest.

One day enough?

Yes, comfortably. Lines rarely exceed 60 minutes outside peak times.

Do I need to speak Korean?

Helpful but not required. Most signage has English. Staff speak limited English but can usually assist.

Best season to visit?

Spring (April-May) for tulip festival and perfect weather. Fall is also excellent.

Everland represents Korean entertainment at its finest: efficient operations, beautiful aesthetics, world-class attractions, and genuine experiences like Lost Valley safari. It's an essential destination for any Korea visitor.
