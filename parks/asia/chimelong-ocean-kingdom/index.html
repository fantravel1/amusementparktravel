‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does it compare to SeaWorld?

Chimelong wins on scale. By a lot.

  • Aquarium: 48M liters vs ~25M
  • Coasters: Legitimate B&M vs modest rides
  • Overall: Chimelong is next-generation

Is it worth the trip from Hong Kong?

Absolutely. 1-hour journey + Macau option + world's largest aquarium = perfect day trip or weekend.

Can I combine with Macau?

Ideal combination:

  • Day 1: Chimelong Ocean Kingdom
  • Day 2: Macau heritage + casinos
  • Optional: Chimelong Paradise (safari park)

One day or two?

One day covers Ocean Kingdom thoroughly. Two days allows Chimelong Paradise safari park + relaxed pace.

Should I worry about language?

Prepare, but don't stress. Download offline Chinese on Google Translate. Most signage has some English. Ride operations are universal.
