# Six Flags Qiddiya: The Complete 2025 Guide

**Six Flags Qiddiya is redefining theme park thrills** with the most record-breaking collection of roller coasters ever assembled in a single park. Located in Saudi Arabia's ambitious Qiddiya Entertainment City, this park represents a $750 million investment in extreme entertainment.

## Why Six Flags Qiddiya Is Historic

Six Flags Qiddiya isn't just another theme park — it's a declaration of intent. With **28 rides including 6 record-breaking roller coasters**, this park will hold more world records than any other theme park on opening day. The Saudi government has invested heavily in making Qiddiya a global entertainment destination, and Six Flags is the crown jewel.

## The Record-Breaking Coasters

### Falcon's Flight — World's Tallest & Fastest

The headline attraction that's rewriting the record books:

- **Height:** 640+ feet (195 meters) — World's Tallest
- **Speed:** 155+ mph (250 km/h) — World's Fastest
- **Drop:** 600+ feet down a natural cliff face
- **Duration:** 3+ minutes of extreme thrills
- **Track Length:** Over 13,000 feet (4 km)

Falcon's Flight uses the natural terrain of Qiddiya's cliff escarpment to create an unprecedented experience. Riders ascend a massive tower, then plunge down a natural cliff face at speeds never before achieved on a roller coaster.

### Sirocco Tower — World's Tallest Drop Tower

- **Height:** 550+ feet (168 meters)
- **Drop:** Freefall from record heights
- **Experience:** 360-degree views of Qiddiya before the plunge

## Themed Areas

### 1. Arabian Summit
The park's main entrance and signature thrills zone, featuring Falcon's Flight and Sirocco Tower with Arabian architectural theming.

### 2. Thrill Oasis
Home to the major coasters and extreme attractions, designed around an oasis theme with modern Arabian influences.

### 3. Family Valley
A gentler area featuring family-friendly rides, live entertainment, and character meet-and-greets appropriate for all ages.

### 4. Desert Adventures
Interactive attractions and mid-level thrills themed around desert exploration and Arabian adventures.

### 5. Sunset Souk
Shopping, dining, and entertainment district with traditional Arabian marketplace theming.

## Best Time to Visit

### Weather Considerations
- **October-April:** Ideal visiting season (70-85°F / 21-29°C)
- **May-September:** Extremely hot (100-115°F / 38-46°C) — evening visits recommended
- **Ramadan:** Limited hours; check calendar

### Crowd Patterns
- **Lowest Crowds:** January-February (weekdays)
- **Moderate:** October-November, March
- **Busiest:** Saudi National Day, Eid holidays, school breaks

**Operating Hours:**
- Regular: 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Summer: 4:00 PM - 1:00 AM (to avoid peak heat)
- Ramadan: After Iftar - 2:00 AM

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need a visa to visit Six Flags Qiddiya?
Yes, most nationalities require a Saudi Arabia tourist visa. The Saudi eVisa is available online for citizens of 49+ countries and typically costs around $120 USD including insurance. Apply at visa.visitsaudi.com.

### Is Six Flags Qiddiya safe for solo female travelers?
Yes. Saudi Arabia has significantly modernized its tourism sector. Women can visit independently, drive rental cars, and enjoy the park without restrictions. Modest dress is required (covering shoulders and knees).

### How does Falcon's Flight compare to other tall coasters?
Falcon's Flight will be approximately 170 feet taller than Kingda Ka (456 ft) and over 20 mph faster than Formula Rossa (149 mph). It's not just breaking records — it's shattering them.

### What is the height requirement for Falcon's Flight?
Expected minimum height is 52 inches (132 cm) due to the extreme forces involved. Final requirements will be confirmed at opening.

### Are there indoor/air-conditioned attractions?
Yes, the park features several indoor attractions and all restaurants and shops are air-conditioned. Queue lines for major attractions also include shaded and air-conditioned sections.

### Can I bring outside food and drinks?
Outside food is generally not permitted, but exceptions may be made for medical dietary needs or baby food. Lockers are available to store items.
