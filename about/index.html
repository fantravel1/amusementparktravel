About AmusementParkTravel

Helping families plan magical theme park adventures around the world

Our Mission

AmusementParkTravel.com exists to fill the gaps in theme park coverage that established sites have overlooked. While others focus exclusively on Disney, we're building the world's most comprehensive resource for all theme parks—from Universal Epic Universe to hidden international gems like Phantasialand and Beto Carrero World.

We believe every family deserves access to the same quality planning information, whether they're visiting Walt Disney World or exploring regional parks like Silver Dollar City, Holiday World, or Dollywood. Our commitment is to provide accurate, up-to-date, actionable information that helps you make the most of your vacation time and budget.

What Makes Us Different

Global Coverage

From Six Flags Qiddiya in Saudi Arabia to Beto Carrero World in Brazil—we cover parks others ignore.

Free Tools

Interactive budget calculators, height checkers, and planning tools that actually help you save money.

Mobile-First

Designed for how you actually plan—on your phone, on the go. Every page loads fast and works perfectly on any device.

Our Coverage Focus

Emerging Parks

First-to-market guides for new parks like Universal Epic Universe, Universal Kids Resort Texas, and Six Flags Qiddiya.

International Destinations

Comprehensive English-language guides for parks that deserve better coverage: Phantasialand (Germany), Beto Carrero World (Brazil), Everland (Korea), and more.

Regional US Parks

Rain policies, height requirements, budget breakdowns, and the practical details that make or break a vacation.
