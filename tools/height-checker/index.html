Height Requirement Tips

Before You Go

  • • Measure your child at home in their park shoes
  • • Heights are measured in shoes at most parks
  • • Some parks offer height wristbands at entrance
  • • Requirements can change — verify day-of

At the Park

  • • Use "Rider Switch" so adults can take turns
  • • Check height at ride entrance before full queue
  • • Don't argue with cast members — it's for safety
  • • Plan backup attractions for "almost there" rides
