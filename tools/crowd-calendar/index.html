Crowd Calendar Tips

Understanding Crowd Levels

  • 1-3: Walk-on to 20 min waits. Best experience.
  • 4-6: 20-45 min waits. Comfortable with strategy.
  • 7-8: 45-90 min waits. Express Pass recommended.
  • 9-10: 90+ min waits. Very challenging day.

  • • School holidays (US, UK, Brazil)
  • • Special events (Halloween, Christmas)
  • • New attraction openings
  • • Weather patterns and seasons
  • • Day of week (weekends busier)
